Threat Landscape

The threat landscape refers to the current types of threats and attacks that organizations and individuals face. It is constantly evolving as attackers develop new methods and defenses improve.

Major threat categories include:

  • Malware: Malicious software designed to damage or exploit systems.
  • Social Engineering: Manipulating humans to disclose information or take harmful actions.
  • Advanced Persistent Threats (APTs): Long-term, targeted campaigns designed to infiltrate and persist in systems.
