Cybersecurity Terminology

Understanding key concepts is crucial when learning cybersecurity. Below are commonly used terms.

  • Asset: Anything of value that needs protection (data, systems, hardware).
  • Threat: A potential cause of an unwanted incident that may harm systems.
  • Vulnerability: A weakness in a system that could be exploited.
  • Exploit: Code or technique that takes advantage of a vulnerability.
  • Risk: The potential for loss or damage from a threat exploiting vulnerability.
